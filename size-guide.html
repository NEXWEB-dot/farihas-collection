How to Measure Your Foot

  1. Place a sheet of paper on a hard floor, against a wall.
  2. Stand on the paper with your heel touching the wall.
  3. Mark the tip of your longest toe on the paper.
  4. Measure the distance from the wall edge to the mark in centimeters.
  5. Match your measurement to the "Foot Length (cm)" column above for your best size.
