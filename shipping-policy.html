Shipping Policy

At Fariha's Collection, we strive to deliver your order safely and on time.

Shipping

  • We ship orders across Pakistan.
  • A standard shipping fee of Rs 350 applies to all orders.
  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after confirmation.
  • Delivery usually takes 2–5 business days, depending on your location.

Payment

  • Karachi: Cash on Delivery (COD) is available.
  • Outside Karachi: Full advance payment is required before dispatch.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive your tracking details via WhatsApp or SMS.

Important Information

  • Please ensure your shipping address and contact number are correct when placing your order.
  • Delivery times may be affected by public holidays, weather conditions, or courier delays.

Parcel Opening

Please record a continuous unboxing video before opening your parcel. Any claim regarding a damaged, missing, incorrect item, or exchange request will only be considered if a clear unboxing video is provided.
